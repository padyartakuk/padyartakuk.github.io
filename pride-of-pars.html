Pride of Pars · Immortal Guard Horse
01 · Curatorial Note

Horse-form movement and ceremonial presence

Pride of Pars carries the horse not as decoration, but as a body of movement, vigilance and symbolic authority. Within the Padyar Takuk practice, the horse becomes a vessel of presence: alert, forward-moving and protective.

The work belongs to a contemporary Persian animal-vessel language. It does not reproduce an ancient object; instead, it gathers memory, motion and metal into a form that can be encountered as both sculpture and takuk/rhyton.

Its force is not loud. It is held in posture, contour, restraint and the quiet tension between animal body and vessel body.

Form

Horse-form animal-vessel body: movement, vessel memory and sculptural presence held together.

Symbol

A work of guardianship, nobility and forward motion within the contemporary language of Padyar Takuk.

Practice

Part of Hadi Hafezi’s Isfahan-based practice in hand-shaped metal and contemporary Persian takuk/rhyton form.

02 · Viewing Room

A quiet encounter with the work

The viewing room keeps the horse-form work in a gallery-like context, allowing the image to carry silhouette, direction, proportion and presence without turning the page into a commercial listing.

03 · Technical Language

Animal and vessel as one discipline

In Padyar Takuk, the horse is not added to a vessel; the animal and the vessel must be read as one body.

This is the central difficulty of the takuk/rhyton form: movement, volume, surface, symbolic identity and vessel logic must remain unified. The work asks the eye to move from animal presence to vessel memory without separating one from the other.

Pride of Pars is a study in motion held still: a horse-form presence shaped into the silence of metal.

04 · فارسی

فخر پارس / اسب نگهبان جاودان

فخر پارس اثری معاصر از زبان پادیار تکوک است؛ اثری که در آن اسب فقط نقش تزئینی ندارد، بلکه به حضور، حرکت، نگهبانی و شأن نمادین تبدیل می‌شود.

در این اثر، اسب و ظرف از هم جدا نیستند. پیکره جانوری، حافظه ظرف را حمل می‌کند و ظرف، حضور اسب را به زبان تکوک/ریتون نزدیک می‌سازد. نگاه مخاطب میان حرکت، وقار، فرم جانوری و سکوت فلز حرکت می‌کند.

فخر پارس عتیقه، شیء باستان‌شناسی، کپی تاریخی یا بازسازی موزه‌ای نیست. این اثر خوانشی معاصر از پیکره جانور-ظرف ایرانی است؛ خوانشی که از مسیر دست، فلز، تناسب و حافظه نمادین شکل گرفته است.

نیروی اثر در فریاد نیست؛ در ایستایی، جهت، کشش فرم و وقار خاموش اسب است.

05 · Documentation

Authenticity and review context

Padyar Takuk works are presented through contemporary-art language and careful documentation. When prepared for private professional review, documentation may include an artwork sheet, certificate of authenticity, not-antiquity statement and process evidence where documented.

The essential clarification remains constant: this is a newly created contemporary artwork by Hadi Hafezi in Isfahan, not an antiquity, archaeological object or historical replica.
